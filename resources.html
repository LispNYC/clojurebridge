Resources

  • Lisp NYC: home page of the workshop organizer, a community dedicated to the advocacy and advancement of Lisp-based development technologies such as Common Lisp, Clojure and Scheme
  • ClojureBridge: home page of the workshop umbrella organization dedicated to increasing diversity within the Clojure community
  • Clojure.org: core resource for Clojure lanugage for necessary software download, documentation and additional learning resources
  • Clojure Meetup NYC: an offline community open to everyone learning Clojure in NYC
  • Lisp NYC Meetup: an offline community for all interested in Lisp-based development technologies, including Clojure. Please join us!
